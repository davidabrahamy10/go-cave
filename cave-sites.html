Here are some of our upcoming destinations

Morka, Turkey

Date: 2022-10-11

The Morka cave located in Erzurum Province, eastern Turkey. This unassuming cave is home to the oldest known rock drawings in the world. More than 18,000 years old, these paintings were made by Paleolithic children and their families who congregated around a campfire inside this ancient and sacred space. Join Us.

Santo Thomas, Cuba

Date: 2022-11-21

Whether you're sailing into the blue depths of the Caribbean, or exploring ancient fossils, Santo Thomas Cave has something for everyone to enjoy. Navigating a maze of waterways is sure to be an adventure any time of day. Here’s a little taste of what awaits you at Santo Thomas Cave, 20ft limestone formations, spelugs that were mined by Archaic period inhabitants and later used by religious figures from the 17th century. Join Us.

Kuredu Caves, Maldives

Date: 2023-12-13

Get lost in the world of blue and green in Kuredu Caves, Maldives. Kuredu Caves are believed to be 1200 years old and the place where the Buddhist pilgrimage is said to have begun.⁣⁣ If you’re into breathtaking views, then this is where you’ve been looking for. Join Us.
